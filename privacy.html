Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 12, 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how CreatorFlow Studio collects, uses, stores, shares, and protects information when you use our website, desktop workspace, documentation, and authorized integrations. We design CreatorFlow Studio to support content planning, drafting, media organization, review, and publishing preparation.

2. Information We Collect

Depending on how you use the service, we may process account contact information, support messages, workspace settings, project metadata, content drafts, media references, integration settings, authorization tokens, diagnostic logs, and usage information needed to operate and secure CreatorFlow Studio.

3. Information From Third-Party Integrations

When you authorize an integration such as TikTok, we only request and process information needed for the features you enable, such as account identity, authorized content workflow data, publishing status, or API permissions granted through the platform's authorization process. We do not use third-party platform data for unrelated advertising or resale.

4. How We Use Information

We use information to provide and maintain the service, operate authorized integrations, prepare content workflows, save user preferences, respond to support requests, prevent abuse, troubleshoot errors, improve reliability, and comply with legal or platform policy obligations.

5. Local and Hosted Data

CreatorFlow Studio is designed with local-first workflows where possible. Some information may remain on your device, while hosted or connected features may require limited processing by our systems or selected third-party providers. The exact storage location depends on the feature, user configuration, and integrations you choose to enable.

6. Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal information. We may share information with service providers that help us operate the service, with third-party platforms you authorize, when required by law, to protect rights and security, or as part of a business transfer subject to appropriate safeguards.

7. AI Providers

If you connect an AI provider or choose an AI-assisted feature, prompts, drafts, project context, or selected media descriptions may be sent to the provider you configure or select. You are responsible for reviewing the provider's terms and privacy practices before enabling that provider.

8. Data Retention

We retain information only for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the service, maintain security, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Locally stored workspace data may remain on your device until you delete it.

10. Security

11. International Use

Your information may be processed in countries or regions where we or our service providers operate. When information is transferred, we use safeguards appropriate to the nature of the data and applicable legal requirements.

12. Children's Privacy

CreatorFlow Studio is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us so we can take appropriate action.
